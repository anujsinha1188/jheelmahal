Jheel Mahal

Udaipur is one the famous destinations to experience the purity of culture and heritage. With various forts and palaces, travelers get a magnificent opportunity to enjoy historical places, gardens, bustling bazaars, and whatnot. Jheel Mahal is situated in the famous area of Mama Ji ki haveli. The property offers a rejuvenating time to guests. As Udaipur is a fusion of great food and regality, the property offers just the right touch of authentic Rajasthani hospitality and treatment to guests. The search for budget-friendly accommodation and stay ends with this hotel. The property offers stunning deals on a 1-night stay and a 2-night stay with a call. Jheel Mahal is well-connected with all the transit points including Udaipur Railway Station (2.00 Km), Local Bus Stand (2.00 Km), Udaipole Bus Stand (2.00 Km), and Maharana Pratap Airport Udaipur (25.00 Km). The property can be conveniently located with the two landmarks - Sagas Ji Bavji Mandir (0.00 Km) and City Palace, Main Gate (0.20 Km). Jheel Mahal offers a comfortable stay with wonderful amenities. The property has a cloakroom, iron boards, and laundry service. To make transit smoother, it has an elevator accessible up to 4 floors. Travelers now enjoy a fully furnished lobby with an air conditioning system and lobby smoke alarm. The property also offers a flexible payment gateway and a fully-stocked pantry. It offers a spacious parking area that can accommodate up to 50 two-wheelers. Some of the best amenities also include a private cab facility and a public washroom. Guests are now welcome to enjoy multi-cuisine dishes at the in-house restaurant. To add to the magnificence, the property has a rooftop caf√©. It also offers top-notch room service, security, and room service. Guests now enjoy complimentary breakfast, Wi-Fi, and branded toiletries. Jheel Mahal has a total room inventory of 10 rooms. The rooms are further divided into 2 Oak rooms, 2 Maple rooms, and 6 Mahogany rooms.
